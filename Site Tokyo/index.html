Top 3 activities to do in Tokyo:

Visit the many pokemon centers:

Dive into the world of Pokemon at the Pokemon Center in Tokyo! Discover exclusive merchandise, participate in exciting events, and meet your favorite Pokemon characters in this vibrant and magical place.

Street Food Tour:

Embark on a culinary adventure through Tokyo's vibrant street food scene. Savor delicious local delicacies like takoyaki, yakitori, and taiyaki, while exploring bustling markets and food stalls.

Visit the temples near Tokyo:

Explore the serene temples near Tokyo, such as the historic Senso-ji in Asakusa and the tranquil Meiji Shrine in Shibuya. Discover the rich cultural heritage and spiritual traditions of Japan.
